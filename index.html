What is MisakaNet

Our flagship distributed learning engine, for Marine and coral multi-omics

Overview

MisakaNet is a privacy-first integration engine designed to bridge the gap between institutional data silos and collective discovery. By utilizing Distributed Learning, MisakaNet allows multiple entities to build high-performance multi-omic models while ensuring that raw multi-omic data never leaves the host institution.

    The Rikoukei Trust Protocol

  • Zero-Data Policy: We do not‚Äîand technically cannot‚Äîstore your raw research data on our local servers. All analysis is performed within your own Local Omics Node (LON).
  • Encrypted Intelligence: MisakaNet incorporates modern privacy-respecting standards and homomorphic encryption to ensure that even the statistical abstractions remain private.
  • Legal Protection: All software and collaborative engagements are protected under UAE Commercial Software Law, providing a robust legal framework for international and domestic partnerships.
  • Data Sovereignty by Design: Institutions retain 100% ownership and control over their datasets. MisakaNet treats your data as a sovereign asset, not a commodity.

Current Applications

  • Red Sea Coral Revival: Managing complex multi-institution data across the Middle East for bleaching risk modeling.
  • Domain-Aware Encoders: Utilizing specialized biological abstraction layers for Cnidarian host and Symbiodiniaceae research.
