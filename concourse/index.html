03 Diner POS App

A till that knows every customer before the tray hits the counter.

A shared-device point of sale for canteen staff, built for the lunch rush, accountable by design.

  • Scan or search. Rotating and static printed codes both work; name search covers the code that won't scan.
  • Balance check before charge: name, role, program, and balance on screen; low-balance warnings, and hard blocks when funds run out.
  • Quick amounts or keypad (5 / 10 / 15 / 20), receipt after every charge, then straight back to scanning.
  • Live daily totals with per-program breakdown, and a printable end-of-day PDF report. Every charge records the staff member who made it.

Security & compliance

Built like ground control:
nothing moves unverified.

Concourse handles identity, attendance, and financial activity for hundreds of people a day, so the security posture is not an afterthought.

Encrypted in transit

All traffic between every app, dashboard, and the server is encrypted end to end in transit. Logging out clears the device session.

Short-lived rotating codes

Meal and attendance QR codes rotate and expire in seconds; attendance codes are single-use. A screenshot is worthless a moment later, and lost printed cards are revoked centrally.

Per-app isolated storage

Each app keeps its session in isolated local storage; one surface can never read another's session. POS customer details are shown only at the moment of a charge, never retained on the device.

Your data stays on your campus

Every institution runs Concourse on its own lightweight, self-hosted infrastructure, so all personal and financial data stays under the university's direct control and inside its own borders, ready to meet local data-residency and protection requirements.
